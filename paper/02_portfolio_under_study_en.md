## 2. Portfolio under study

**Question:** What are we analyzing?

The object of study is a 60/40 portfolio defined by constant target weights: 60% equity and 40% fixed income. Within each asset class, allocation is equal-weighted. Weights are interpreted as a static capital structure for diagnosis; weight drift, buy-and-hold dynamics, and periodic rebalancing are not modeled.

The portfolio combines broad market indexes, technology and sector exposure, individual equities, and fixed income, in order to represent a plausible allocation for an individual investor.
