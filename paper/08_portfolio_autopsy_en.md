**Central figure.** Progressive portfolio diagnosis.

The funnel summarizes the empirical progression: ten nominal instruments shrink only slightly when capital concentration is measured (9.55), more sharply when percentage risk-contribution concentration is measured (6.58), and—on the common-factor plane—most joint variability (80%) is captured by three principal components.
